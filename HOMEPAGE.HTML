Key Features of MatruMitra

  • Comprehensive Maternal and Child Health Tracking — monitors health status of pregnant women, mothers, and children from conception to early childhood.
  • Digital Registration and Data Management — enables online registration of beneficiaries with unique IDs for accurate tracking.
  • Antenatal, Postnatal, and Child Health Records — maintains complete medical history including ANC, PNC, immunization, and growth monitoring.
  • Automated Alerts and Reminders — sends reminders for scheduled check-ups, vaccinations, and follow-up visits.
  • Dashboard and Analytics — provides real-time data visualization for maternal and child health indicators.
